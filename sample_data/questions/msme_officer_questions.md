# MSME Officer Chat Questions (Food Processing + FSSAI)

## Policy-grounded risk questions

1. Based on FSSAI context and applicant details, what are the top 5 approval risks?
2. Which risks are regulatory vs operational vs financial?
3. Which findings require immediate manual escalation?
4. Is the current profile low, medium, or high risk and why?

## Decision-support questions

1. What covenants should be added before sanction?
2. Which mitigation actions can reduce risk from high to medium?
3. What evidence should the applicant provide in the next review cycle?
4. What is missing for a confident approval recommendation?

## Explainability and citation questions

1. Cite guideline points used to classify this profile.
2. Which applicant statements most strongly influenced the score?
3. If DSCR improves to 1.35 and traceability reaches 95 percent, what changes?
4. Provide a concise recommendation note for the credit committee.
