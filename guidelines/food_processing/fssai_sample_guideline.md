# FSSAI Food Processing Guideline (Sample)

- Verify valid FSSAI license and category mapping for product lines.
- Review last 12 months food safety inspection observations and closures.
- Confirm HACCP-like control plan, sanitation SOPs, and CCP monitoring logs.
- Ensure batch traceability for raw material to finished goods.
- Validate label compliance and allergen declarations per FSSAI requirements.
- Escalate if critical non-compliance remains open beyond remediation timeline.
