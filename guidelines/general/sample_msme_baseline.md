# MSME Baseline Guideline (Sample)

- DSCR below 1.2 indicates elevated repayment risk.
- Net profit margin below 5 percent for 3+ quarters increases risk.
- GST filing delays for more than 2 periods are a warning indicator.
- Bounced cheque frequency above threshold is a high risk signal.
- Collateral coverage above 1.25x reduces risk category by one band if cash flow is stable.
